# Supply_chain-dashboard
Supply Chain Optimization &amp; Demand Forecasting Dashboard ​An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to monitor, analyze, and optimize supply chain operations. This project leverages data-driven insights to evaluate supplier performance, product stock metrics, shipping costs, and profitability dynamics across various regional hubs. 

​
**Key Features & Insights:**

​
**Executive Summary Metrics**: Tracks high-level KPIs including Total Revenue , Net Profit Margin , and Average Lead Time .

​**Inventory Management**: Evaluates ordered quantities against current stock levels across core product types (Haircare, Skincare, Cosmetics).

​**Supplier Quality Analysis**: Visualizes defect rates per supplier to identify vulnerabilities in the vendor pipeline.

​**Logistics & Transport Efficiency**: Compares various transport modes (Road, Air, Rail, Sea) by intersecting shipping costs against transit times.

​**Geographical Profitability**: Analyzes net profit performance across major distribution hubs, including Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore, and Delhi.


**Tech Stack:**

​**Business Intelligence: Power BI Desktop**

​**Data Transformation: Power Query (ETL)**

​**Modeling & Metrics: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
